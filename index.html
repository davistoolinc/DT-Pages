Davis Tool, Inc.

Who We Are.

In 1994 Neal and Penny Davis opened Davis Tool Inc., designing and building intricate injection molds and dies. Over the years, our multi-generational company has expanded its team and capabilities to include plastic injection molding, metal and ceramic injection molding (MIM and CIM), sintering, and small tonnage stamping.

Our mission has been to treat others how we would like to be treated by providing personal, on-time service, and good value. Our hard-working and cohesive team works closely with our customers, acting as an extension of their business in order to fulfill or exceed their needs and expectations.

Over 29 years later, the name of our company is still a statement that the work done here is worthy of our family's reputation.
